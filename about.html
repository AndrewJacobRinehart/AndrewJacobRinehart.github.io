Andrew Jacob Rinehart (he/him)

interdisciplinary artist / music educator

I am an interdisciplinary artist and music educator. I work with a variety of mediums including sound, circuits, found objects, and with additive and subtractive technologies like 3D-printing, and laser-cutting. I'm interested in the quiet, still, and under-observed nuances of daily life. I often engage the public through chance encounters, with whimsical installations that transform liminal spaces. Currently I am exploring new methods of engagement through the web, and in physical space. When I'm not working on my own creations, I'm nurturing budding creatives from my home studio, where I teach music through the harp, and creative technologies.


I am grateful to reside on the Haldimand Tract, traditional lands of the Anishnaabeg, Haudenosaunee, and Neutral peoples, in what is now known as Waterloo.
