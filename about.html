Andrew Jacob Rinehart (he/they)

interdisciplinary artist / music educator

I am a harp player, music educator, curator, and interdisciplinary artist, working with experimental events, installation, painting, and sound. Having previously used new technology as a cornerstone of my practice, I am now entirely skeptical of the digital, and its implications for our physical, social, spiritual, and environmental wellbeing. These days I am looking to my Ukrainian heritage to redefine my practice, re-skill, and remember art making practices of the past that were fundamentally in harmony with Earth. My work is about having good relationships; with tools and materials, friends and neighbours, plants and animals, and, land and water.
